About The Author

Percival Mariano, Percy for short, graduated from Highline College with the degrees of A.A.S. in Respiratory Care and A.A.S. in Polysomnographic Technology. He is a holder of a Registered Respiratory Therapist (RRT) credential issued by the National Board for Respiratory Care and a Washington State license for Respiratory Care Practitioners as issued by the Washington State Department of Health. Likewise, he is a credentialed Polysomnographic Technologist with both the Board of Registered Polysomnographic Technologists and the American Board of Sleep Medicine.

His experience includes various clinical rounds involving neonatal and pediatric as well as adult acute cardiopulmonary critical care management in the confines of Emergency Departments and Intensive Care Units among several hospitals within the State. He also worked as the Assistant Manager for Respiratory Care Services for ALH Healthcare at the Rainier Rehabilitation facility in Puyallup before becoming a full time Polysomnoraphic Technologist at the UW Medicine Valley Medical Center.

Whenever he gets a time off, he oftentimes spends it day-trading stocks but only in the morning from 6:30 a.m. to 9:00 a.m. while the rest of the day is spent on family and friends.

Right now, Percy is working on becoming a Full Stack Web Developer and is currently undergoing training in Seattle. He is a diligent and conscientious worker; down-to-earth, courteous, compassionate, friendly and most of all, great to work with. Percy would be very much eager to contribute to your team as well as help complete whatever goals your team envisions to accomplish.
